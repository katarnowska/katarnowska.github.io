I am a research scientist and computer systems engineer, interested in investigating AI/ML-based approaches to augment decision-making with Recommender Systems/Decision Support System technology. I received a Ph.D. in Computing and Information Systems under the supervision of Dr. Zbigniew Ras from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte in 2018. I hold a double M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Technical University of Berlin and Warsaw University of Technology (2015). I have an Engineer's degree from Warsaw University of Technology (2013) and a Master's degree in Quantitative Methods in Economics and Information Systems from Warsaw School of Economics (2011). I have 4+ academic experience, as Assistant Professor at SJSU-CS Dept. and Research/Teaching Assistant at UNCC-KDD lab, and 3+ industry experience as an IT consultant and programmer (Oracle, Pentacomp, Ernst & Young, Bosch). This is my full academic CV and shorter resume.

Research interests

  • Recommender systems and decision support systems: knowledge-based systems, health informatics, business analytics, information security

  • Knowledge discovery in databases: actionable knowledge mining, machine learning, big data, data visualization

  • Artificial intelligence: knowledge reasoning, logic programming, expert systems, natural language processing
